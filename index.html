6Pets & animals

If pets will live here, describe each one — type, breed, and weight. Breed is required for dogs. (No pets? Skip this.)

For insurance and safety reasons, certain dog breeds are not permitted in our homes — including pit bull-type breeds (American Pit Bull Terrier, American Staffordshire Terrier, Staffordshire Bull Terrier), Rottweilers, Doberman Pinschers, Cane Corso, Presa Canario, Akitas, Chow Chows, wolf-hybrids, and any mix of these. This applies to pets only.
Service and support animals are not pets. They're never charged a pet fee, and breed, weight, and size restrictions do not apply to them — this includes every breed listed above. We ask only so we can accommodate you properly.
Minnesota note — service & support animals (Minn. Stat. § 504B.113). Service and support animals are not pets. Under the Minnesota Human Rights Act and Minn. Stat. § 504B.113, they are handled as reasonable accommodations for a disability, and the breed, weight, and size limits above do not apply. We may request reliable documentation of the disability-related need; we will never ask for your medical records. Knowingly misrepresenting an animal as a service or support animal, or providing fraudulent documentation, is unlawful under Minn. Stat. § 504B.113 and may be grounds for denial.

8Deposits & holding a unit

Submitting this pre-screen does not reserve the unit, and it does not start any credit or background check.

Security deposit. A standard security deposit (typically about one month's rent) is due at lease signing, handled securely through our resident portal. We don't offer split or monthly deposit plans.

Want to hold a unit? Serious applicants can place a $300 refundable holding fee to take a unit off the market. If you're approved, it applies toward your deposit; if you're not approved, it's refunded in full. Once we connect, we'll send you our business Venmo (@greenkeypropertiesmn) to place it — no payment is collected on this form.
